Day 1

Bari — Charming Old Town & First Bites

Nonna pasta, seafront sparkle & focaccia heaven

Land in Bari, grab your car, and step into the prettiest old town. Wander hand-in-hand through tiny streets, watch Nonnas hand-make orecchiette, and feel that instant Italian magic wash over you.

Girls' Must-Dos
  • Street-food crawl: hot focaccia, panzerotti, sgagliozze — perfect for sharing & snapping
  • Basilica di San Nicola for golden-hour pics and moody arches
  • Sunset passeggiata along the seafront in your cutest outfit
Boutique hotel or stylish B&B in Bari center

Day 2

Polignano a Mare — Cliffside Dream Beach Day

Turquoise coves, mermaid swims & spritz o'clock

Just a quick drive to one of Italy's most photogenic spots. Dive into turquoise water framed by dramatic white cliffs — pure mermaid vibes that'll have your camera roll overflowing.

Girls' Must-Dos
  • Golden-hour photos from the bridge overlooking Lama Monachile beach
  • Boat tour into sea caves for cinematic cave-swim content
  • Fresh seafood lunch with sea views — Aperol spritz mandatory
  • Stroll the poetic white streets and pose with the "Volare" statue
Clifftop hotel in Polignano — wake up to ocean views

Day 3

Alberobello & Wine — Trulli Fairy-Tale Land

Whitewashed lanes, rosé tastings & poolside aperitivo

Enter fairy-tale land: cone-roofed trulli houses everywhere you look. Then retreat to your private masseria for pool time and wine under ancient olive trees. This is Puglia at its dreamiest.

Girls' Must-Dos
  • Wander Rione Monti for the prettiest trulli selfies you'll ever take
  • Primitivo wine tasting at a vineyard — rosé & burrata pairing goals
  • Poolside aperitivo at your masseria in a flowy dress

Day 4

Matera — Sunset in the Ancient Caves

Cave city, dusk glow & prosecco overlooking the ravine

Drive to the most magical place you've ever seen: an entire cave city glowing at dusk. It feels like a movie set built for golden-hour content — because James Bond actually filmed here.

Girls' Must-Dos
  • Sunset aperitivo overlooking the ravine — prosecco is essential
  • Explore glowing cave streets for dreamy night photos
  • Book a table at a cave restaurant for an unforgettable dinner
Cave hotel in the Sassi — sleep like ancient royalty

Day 5

Full Matera Day — Cave Churches & Panoramas

UNESCO wonder day — frescoes, hidden paths & cave bars

All day to fall in love with this UNESCO wonder: rock-carved churches, hidden paths, and epic viewpoints in every direction. Take it slow — there's magic in every corner.

Girls' Must-Dos
  • Guided tour of frescoed cave churches for full goosebumps
  • Hike to panoramic spots for the ultimate group selfie
  • Sunset drinks in a cave bar — pure, unforgettable magic

Day 6

Naples — Pizza Heaven & Vibrant Streets

The birthplace of pizza, limoncello & pure energy

Arrive in the birthplace of pizza — eat, shop, and wander colorful alleys like locals. Naples hits you with raw, beautiful energy unlike anywhere else in Italy.

Girls' Must-Dos
  • Pizza pilgrimage: Da Michele or Sorbillo — share multiple pies
  • Stroll Spaccanapoli & Quartieri Spagnoli for street-style inspo
  • Evening limoncello or sfogliatella treat before crashing

Naples Morning Glow & Fly Home

Espresso, sea views & one last pastry

Last morning: espresso in a historic café, pastry in hand, sea views in front of you. Depart feeling recharged, sun-kissed, and totally radiant. Arrivederci, bella!
